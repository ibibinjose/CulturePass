# About CulturePass

> Last reviewed: May 8, 2026.

## What Is CulturePass?

CulturePass is a cross-platform lifestyle and community platform built for cultural diaspora communities. It helps people discover events, connect with their communities, support local cultural businesses, and celebrate the richness of their heritage — wherever they call home.

The platform brings together cultural events, community hubs, business directories, ticketing, and loyalty rewards into a single, beautifully designed experience available on iOS, Android, and web.

---

## The Mission

> **Helping diaspora communities stay connected to their culture, to each other, and to the places that matter.**

CulturePass was created to solve a real gap: diaspora communities are vibrant and active, but their events, businesses, and gathering spaces are scattered across social media, word-of-mouth, and niche websites. CulturePass unifies them in one place — a cultural home you carry in your pocket.

---

## Who It's For

CulturePass serves three overlapping audiences:

| Audience | What They Get |
|---|---|
| **Community Members** | Discover events, join communities, earn perks, buy tickets |
| **Event Organisers & Artists** | Publish events, sell tickets, reach a targeted cultural audience |
| **Cultural Businesses & Venues** | List in the directory, build reputation, attract community foot traffic |

---

## Where We Operate

CulturePass is built for diaspora communities worldwide. The platform supports any city — users see events and communities relevant to their location first.

Current focus cities include Sydney, Melbourne, Auckland, Dubai, London, and Toronto, with new cities added as organiser and community density grows. There is no geographic restriction on sign-up, event creation, or business listings.

---

## Key Features

### Discover
Browse and search cultural events filtered by city, category, date, and culture tag. Featured events are surfaced through an editorial aurora-style hero banner. Events carry culture tags (e.g. South Asian, East African, Pacific Islander, Indigenous Australian) so users can find what resonates.

### Community Hub
Join diaspora communities — cultural groups, associations, collectives, and fan clubs. Members can connect, share, and coordinate around shared identity and interests.

### Ticketing
Purchase event tickets directly in-app. Each ticket generates a QR code for venue check-in. Ticket history and upcoming events surface in the Profile tab.

### Business & Venue Directory
Find cultural restaurants, grocery stores, fashion boutiques, beauty salons, and event venues run by and for diaspora communities. Profiles include photos, ratings, location, and community endorsements.

### First Nations Spotlight
A dedicated space celebrating Indigenous Australian culture — art, events, artists, and community voices. CulturePass is committed to amplifying First Nations perspectives as a core part of the platform, not a footnote.

### Loyalty Perks
Earn and redeem perks across the platform. Members accumulate rewards through attendance, purchases, and community engagement. Higher membership tiers unlock greater cashback and exclusive access.

## Membership Tiers

CulturePass offers a tiered membership model designed to reward deeper engagement:

| Tier | Description |
|---|---|
| **Free** | Full event discovery, basic community access |
| **Plus** | Enhanced perks, early ticket access |
| **Elite** | Priority listings, increased cashback |
| **Pro** | Organiser tools, analytics, promoted events |
| **Premium** | VIP event access, exclusive cultural experiences |
| **VIP** | Top-tier rewards, partner benefits, white-glove support |

Membership is managed via Stripe subscriptions with monthly and annual billing options. Tier status syncs in real time via Firebase custom claims.

## The CulturePass Vision

We believe culture is not something you leave behind when you move — it travels with you. It shapes how you cook, celebrate, grieve, worship, dance, and gather. CulturePass exists to make those moments findable, shareable, and accessible to every member of the diaspora, no matter which city they're in.

The long-term vision is a global cultural OS: a living, searchable map of diaspora culture — events, businesses, artists, venues, and communities — continuously enriched by the people who live it.

## Design Philosophy

CulturePass has a distinctive visual identity built around the idea of **cultural vibrancy meets modern digital craft**:

- **Aurora gradient** (blue → purple → coral) reflects the spectrum of cultures on the platform
- **Glassmorphism surfaces** give a premium, contemporary feel without sterile minimalism
- **Gold accents** signal premium status and celebration
- **Sunset gradients** (coral → orange → gold) evoke warmth and community
- **Neon highlights** (blue, purple, teal) are used sparingly for interactive focal points

The design adapts to both light and dark mode, prioritising legibility and a sense of place.
